I am a director of software engineering at SambaNova Systems where I lead the machine learning team. My work focuses on building state-of-the-art machine learning systems. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Stanford University where I studied the intersection of graph, database, and machine learning systems under the direction of Christopher Ré and Kunle Olukotun; this work received a Best Of award at VLDB and an invited TODS article.
